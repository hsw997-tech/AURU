About AURU

AURU explores jewelry as quiet art. Founded in Berlin by an independent designer, each piece is handcrafted from silver, ceramic, and natural stone. Imperfection is embraced as part of the beauty.

All items are made in small batches. We value traceable materials, tactile finishes, and long-lasting design.
